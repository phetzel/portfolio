A quick bio

I am a native of the east bay area who loves nature, eating as many different types of amazing foods as possible, and of lately coding! I have spent the majority of the last decade working various jobs, primarily in the service industry, where, while I am very grateful for all my employers and the lovely people who I have met, I had started to feel unchallenged and complacent. A few years ago I discovered coding, where learning how to build things made me excited again!

I finally decided to go all in on coding and have spent the last 6 months sharpining my skills. I graduated the App ACademy boot camp in 2020, and am finally in a place where I feel comfortable about puting myself out there. I am a hardworking, optimistic employee, who is used to working with a team to do whatever it takes to make a buisness successful.

Thanks for taking the time to check me out!
